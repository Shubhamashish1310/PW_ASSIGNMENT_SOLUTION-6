MS Dhoni: Former Indian captain and wicket-keeper batsman, led India to victory in the 2007 T20 World Cup, 2011 ODI World Cup, and 2013 Champions Trophy.

Virat Kohli: Former Indian captain and batsman, holds the record for most runs in international cricket, most centuries in Test and ODI cricket, and most centuries as captain in all three formats.

Ravindra Jadeja: All-rounder, holds the record for most wickets taken by a left-arm spinner in Test cricket and most catches taken by a fielder in Test cricket.

Rohit Sharma: Current Indian captain and batsman, holds the record for most runs in T20 Internationals, most centuries in T20 Internationals, and most double centuries in ODIs.

Ruturaj Gaikwad: Batsman, holds the record for most runs scored in a single IPL season and most centuries scored in a single IPL season.

Jasprit Bumrah: Bowler, holds the record for fastest bowler to take 50 wickets in both ODIs and Tests.
